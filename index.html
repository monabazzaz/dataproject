GLBAL ALCOHOL CONSUMPTION

Annual alcohol consumption has been tracked in approximately 40 countries over the past five decades. The Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development defines alcohol consumption as annual sales of pure alcohol in liters per person ages 15 and older.

The map below represents alcohol consumption in the year 2013 for the 34 nations in which data was collected. The darker the color of a nation, the greater the liters per capita.
